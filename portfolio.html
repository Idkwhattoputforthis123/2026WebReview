About Me

Get to know the developer behind the code

My name is Andrew and I was born on September 8th 2009 at Canton Potsdam Hospital. I have a Mother and a Father named Christine and Matthew.
I have a sister named Katelynn. And I had a brother named Ryan who passed away in 2018. I also had a Grandmother named Carol who also died in 2018.
My technology experience is very new and I hope to expand it. So far It has been okay. My inspiration for computer science is just my interest in computers and how important they will be in the future.
I have a great dane right now named Cooper who I love and hold very dear to me. He has helped me in filling in the spots of my brother and grandmother who were very close to me.
My early school years were not the best. I have had two teachers in particular who would make me feel very small and very insecure. I had another teacher who ended up making me a blanket and gave it to me at my brother's funeral. I went and have always gone to Norwood Norfolk-Central School. And I don’t recall having any long term friendships with anyone in elementary school. Before eighth grade it was very hard for me to make friends. My friendships would only last a few months at most and one in particular lasted only three days.
When I made it to eighth grade I met one of the most special people in my life. Her name is Aubree and I met her in 2022. Our friendship has lasted for about four years now as there have been many fights but also many special moments.
I was never very involved in sports after elementary school but I did used to play baseball, hockey, and basketball. I was never involved in any sort of clubs however. Right now my future goals are unclear. I have a lot of choices I want to pick from in a job path, mainly three in particular. My first choice would be something in computer science related. Secondly something in mental health and lastly a specific job would be architecture. I plan on using the skills I have learned in high school and applying them to going to college for something computer science related.
I see myself in 10 years having my permanent job. I see myself also being a lot better version of myself than I am right now. I feel like its very unclear as how my future is going to go but I plan on navigating through the fog of it all.
